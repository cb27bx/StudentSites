About

I have been boxing on and off since I was a young child. Throughout my training I have realized just how much equipment I use an spend money on.

I always thought it would be nice if I could get my gear and do research about it on the same website instead of having to bounce around from review pages to online stores.

The idea for this site came from struggling to find accurate reviews of quality boxing gear and equipment for me, my coaches, and my teammates. I would like to simplify the review and purchasing process.

This website's goal is to be the Amazon of the Boxing world!
